About Us

Supreme Enterprise is a leading manufacturing company based in Benin City, Edo State. Located at 124 Sapele Road in the heart of Oredo, we have built our reputation on delivering exceptional manufacturing solutions that meet the highest standards of quality and precision.

Our state-of-the-art facility combines cutting-edge technology with experienced craftsmanship to serve businesses across Nigeria. We understand the unique demands of the Nigerian market and tailor our manufacturing processes to deliver products that exceed expectations.
